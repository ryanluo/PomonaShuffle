The Pomona Shuffle

The Pomona Shuffle is an interactive class-finder for the eager, wise, and slightly area-deficient Sagehen. Pressing space brings up ten new courses currently offered (Fall 2013) for you to peruse at yoru leisure!

Pomona Shuffle is currently displaying all 1500 courses available to all students at the 5Cs. Come back next semester for more updates!
